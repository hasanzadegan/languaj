What is languaj.com?

languaj.com is an IT platform for easy creation of language contents by teachers as language courses. each courses can share easily and used with language learners. They are consist of small peace of lessons and tests which mixed together called quizlet. phrases repeated in different types of quizlet. as a learner don't worry about making mistakes. just go on and learn like children.
